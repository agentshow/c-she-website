About C Community

C Community was born from a core conviction: the AI era has brought a leap in productivity, but it has also intensified the inequality of resources and opportunities. Ordinary people, if they do not proactively organize, will remain in a passive position amidst technological transformation. C Community's response is not confrontation, but constructive alliance — centering on real commercial projects, enabling individuals to enhance their abilities, share resources, and gain returns through collaboration.
