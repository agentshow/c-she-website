OPC Trend -- The One-Person Company Era

Global tech leaders are predicting the same thing: AI will make one-person companies the mainstream form of entrepreneurship.

01

Sam Altman

OpenAI CEO

"We have an internal bet about what year we'll see the first one-person billion-dollar company. That was unimaginable before AI, but now it's essentially inevitable."

02

Dario Amodei

Anthropic CEO

At the 2025 Code with Claude developer conference, when asked "When will the world see a one-person billion-dollar company?", he answered directly: "2026."

03

Vinod Khosla

Renowned venture capitalist, founder of Khosla Ventures

A long-term investor in the one-person company space, Khosla believes AI will fundamentally reshape the organizational form of entrepreneurship, enabling individuals to gain capabilities that previously required entire teams.

04

Robin Li

Founder of Baidu

"The smallest unit of productivity has become a super individual -- one person plus a fleet of AI agents. We are entering an era where super individuals rise, and everyone can be a super individual."

-- 2025 Baidu World Conference keynote, "The Emergence of Effectiveness"

05

Zhou Hongyi

Founder of 360 Group

"One-person companies are not about slacking off in entrepreneurship, but rather an extreme expression of super individuals and super teams in the AI era. As AI agent technology becomes widespread, this model lowers the barrier to entrepreneurship, enabling more people to launch commercial projects in a lean way."

06

Andrey Kholkin

Founder of Weberlo

After the release of ChatGPT, his one-person SaaS company Weberlo achieved customer service workloads that previously required an entire team, proving that AI's empowerment of solo entrepreneurs is real and immediately available.
