Core Projects

C Community has built an interconnected project ecosystem. Four core projects cover AI capability building, personal entrepreneurship, study abroad services, and content distribution. Each operates independently while deeply collaborating on resources and traffic. Members can participate in one or more projects based on their needs.

01

SAT

Learn AI Together

A practical-oriented AI capability enhancement program. No chasing trends, no selling anxiety — committed to the methodology of "learn for use, apply what you learn, create value through application."

Learn about SAT
02

Onmyown

Personal Entrepreneurship Platform

An entrepreneurship empowerment platform built for the OPC (One-Person Company) era. Providing three layers of support — AI tools, community-driven promotion channels, and the C-Store marketplace — helping individuals achieve "sovereign independence with ecological collaboration."

Learn about Onmyown
03

Cloudex

Cloudex Study Abroad

A DIY-focused study abroad support system. Redefining the application process as "your first self-directed project," using AI tools and a real experience database to help students take full control of their application journey.

Learn about Cloudex
04

Agentshow

Agentshow

C Community's media matrix infrastructure. Solving the three major bottlenecks of individual content creators — content production, sustained operations, and traffic acquisition — while providing distribution capabilities for the C Community ecosystem.

Learn about Agentshow

A Mutually Supporting Ecosystem

The four projects are not isolated business lines, but interconnected ecosystem nodes. SAT provides the AI capability foundation for all projects; Onmyown's C-Store offers transaction scenarios for Cloudex and Agentshow; Cloudex and Agentshow in turn provide traffic entry points for Onmyown entrepreneurs. One member can participate in multiple projects simultaneously, gaining compound value through cross-participation.
