Cloudex

Study Abroad Support

Cloudex is C Community's study abroad support program with a core philosophy of DIY -- redefining the application process as "your first self-directed project." Traditional study abroad service models suffer from serious information asymmetry and conflicts of interest. Cloudex uses AI tool systems and real experience databases to help students take control of the entire application process and reduce dependence on agencies.

Two Core Offerings

AI Tool System

University admissions data collection, essay writing assistance, application process management, and other essential DIY application tools.

Real Experience Database

Authentic application experiences, university information, and study experiences contributed by international students and alumni. Far more valuable than agency marketing materials.

Three-Party Participants

Cloudex builds a collaborative system with three roles: students receive authentic application support, international students and alumni earn returns by sharing their experiences, and promoters earn income by spreading Cloudex's philosophy.

DIY Philosophy

Study abroad applications are not a transactional process to "hand off to an agency," but the first important self-directed project on a young person's growth journey. DIY is not about saving money -- it's about growth. Through the complete experience of research, school selection, essay writing, and interview preparation, you gain the ability to independently plan your life.

Cloudex does not replace students' decisions but provides tools and experience support: AI tools improve efficiency, and the real experience database provides navigation. The final decision-making power and execution authority always remain in the student's hands.

Full-Cycle Support

From study abroad decisions, school selection, and document preparation to visa processing and overseas adaptation, Cloudex provides full-cycle support. Rather than making decisions for students, it helps them gain the ability and information to make better decisions.
