Cloudex DIY Philosophy Three Participants

Three Participants

Based on Cloudex's positioning, the community provides value realization channels for three types of roles. Each party takes on different roles and receives different value returns, together forming a sustainably operating ecosystem.

Three Roles and Their Value

Students

Students first enter the Cloudex community as users, completing study abroad applications in a DIY manner, gaining support from AI tools and an experience database throughout the process, achieving capability growth.

After completing their applications, students transition to an international student identity and can continue participating in community building as experience sharers, earning contribution reward points and promotion commissions through sharing experiences and participating in promotions, growing from value consumers into value creators.

International Students / Alumni

International students are the most core experience contributors of the Cloudex community. Cloudex has designed a contribution point reward system that provides redeemable point returns for contribution behaviors such as experience sharing, tutoring, and content creation, institutionalizing mutually beneficial actions.

A single international student's experience may seem insignificant, but the distributed experiences of many international students, when pooled together, form a comprehensive, authentic, and continuously updated information network -- a service capability that no centralized intermediary institution could possibly replicate.

Promoters

All C Community members have the right to participate in Cloudex's development and can earn promotion commissions by recommending Cloudex services to families around them with study abroad needs. This is the direct embodiment of C Community's co-creation and sharing model in official commercial projects.

Beyond Study Abroad Applications

Cloudex's service scope extends beyond study abroad applications. Cloudex's long-term positioning is to build the best environment for student growth and development -- from pre-study-abroad preparation, to academic and campus life during studies, to career and professional development after graduation, providing full-cycle support and companionship. Every kind of growth requires a healthy, well-structured environment with incentive mechanisms, and Cloudex is committed to becoming that environment.
