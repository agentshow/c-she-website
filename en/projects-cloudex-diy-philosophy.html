DIY Philosophy

Cloudex's core philosophy is DIY (Do It Yourself). We redefine the study abroad application as a "self-directed project" -- it should not be an assembly-line product outsourced to agencies, but rather the first major project management practice that students complete on their own.

Breaking Information Barriers

The core competitive advantage of traditional study abroad agencies is not professional expertise, but information asymmetry -- they control university information, application strategies, and visa rules that students and parents do not understand. Cloudex's mission is to break down these information barriers, enabling students to access more authentic information at lower cost.

Cloudex provides two core offerings: an AI tool system and a real experience database. AI tools help students efficiently complete specific tasks such as school selection analysis and essay optimization. The real experience database offers first-hand information from international students and alumni, helping students make better decisions.

A study abroad application is your first self-directed project -- its significance lies not only in gaining admission, but in gaining the ability to take control of your own life.
