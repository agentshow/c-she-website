Onmyown

Entrepreneurship Platform

Onmyown is C Community's entrepreneurship empowerment platform built for the One-Person Company (OPC) era. In China, the number of individual businesses has reached 124 million; in the United States, 84% of businesses are non-employer firms. The one-person company is not a niche phenomenon, but an accelerating structural trend. Onmyown's goal is to enable every willing individual to launch and run their own venture with "sovereign independence and ecological collaboration."

Three-Layer Empowerment System

01

AI Empowerment

Through SAT's practical learning paths, we help entrepreneurs master AI tools. AI dramatically lowers the skill barrier for individual entrepreneurship -- one person with AI can accomplish what previously required a whole team.

02

Community-Driven Promotion

One of the biggest challenges for individual entrepreneurs is customer acquisition. Onmyown leverages C Community's social network and Agentshow's content matrix to provide low-cost promotion channels. Mutual recommendations among community members create a natural trust endorsement.

03

C-Store Marketplace

C-Store is Onmyown's marketplace, providing entrepreneurs with product showcase and trade matching services. Whether physical goods or digital services, everything can be listed on C-Store. C-Store serves not only Onmyown entrepreneurs but also functions as the transaction infrastructure for Cloudex and Agentshow.

Sovereign independence, ecological collaboration -- individuals retain full autonomy while enjoying the collaborative dividends of the community ecosystem.
